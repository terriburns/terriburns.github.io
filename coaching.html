What even is coaching?

Coaching is a powerful, ongoing relationship that helps you focus to better realize your visions and goals. With a professional coach, you get the tools and support to remove obstacles and facilitate change. Some examples of reasons people come to coaching: to plan a career move, to make your present job more fulfilling, to plan your life based on your true values, to get more organized, to focus your energy, to finish or plan a project.

FAQ

How is coaching done?

Typically, individual coaching is done by telephone or video conference, with two, hourlong sessions each month. To create profound changes in your life, I ask for a minimum six-session commitment.

Is coaching therapy?

Nope. Coaching focuses on personal growth, strategic planning, and does not attempt to heal emotional trauma. Coaches regularly refer clients to other professionals for issues that are better suited for a therapeutic relationship.

What is a typical session like?

The hallmark of coaching is self-responsibility. The client helps set the agenda for the call, which the coach helps guide. You can expect transformational questions, focused planning, and accountability tools.
