Tomorrow

- December 31st, 2019 -

Tomorrow, I’m more in love with myself.
My heart is open and prepared
free to love others more deeply
scars still visible
though they’ve matured.
There is room for more of them.

Tomorrow, I’m a pleasure activist.
I’m centering joy in my quest for justice
luxury on my list of motivations
money as the root of evil and of liberation
I’m carrying those around me in ecstasy and with laughter.

Tomorrow, I expand.
I’m no longer building
that was yesterday.
I thought that tomorrow might be for growing, for ascension.
But with expansion comes spaciousness and freedom
and spaciousness and freedom are what I need.

Tomorrow!
